Deploying OpenVNFManager on OpenStack:

Overview:

This Project was done as a part of my Internship at Tata Consultancy Services during Summer 2015.

Integrated various software components of OpenVNFManager on OpenStack and turned up the vIMS VoIP service in OpenStack Tenant network.

Tools:

  • OpenStack
  • Red Hat Enterprise Linux

Deployment:

  • Installed Red Hat Enterprise Linux on Intel Xeon servers.
  • Deployed multi node OpenStack Juno cloud on the servers.
  • Integrated NFV Manager service similar to default services such as Neutron, Nova onto OpenStack.
  • Orchestrated vIMS Project Clearwater VMs on a tenant. On completion, VoIP calls could be made between two softphones from one network to another inside the OpenStack tenant.
