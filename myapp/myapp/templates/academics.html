  • Since the course is Interdisciplinary between Computer Science and Electrical Engineering, I have a good understanding of topics from both streams.
  • Some of the courses which I took during my tenure at UTD are;
    • Advanced Computer Networks

      Concepts such as TCP/IP, SCTP, Broadcast, Routing, Router Design, MPLS, QoS, Mobile IP, P2P systems were learnt.

    • Software Defined Networks

      Learnt about the concepts such as disruption caused by SDN in industry, SDN controllers, OpenFlow protocol, Use cases.

      Course involved the study of efficient algorithms for various computational problems. Algorithm design techniques. Sorting and manipulation of data structures, graphs. Complexity of algorithms, lower bounds, NP-Completeness.

      Learnt about Queuing models and physical origin of random variables used in queuing models, various important cases of the M/M/m/N queuing system, The M/G/1 queuing system, product form solutions of open and closed queuing networks, Discrete time queuing systems. Also, programmed simulations of queuing systems.

    • Telecommunications Software Design

      Course main focus was on understanding fundamental challenges in telecommunication software systems such as concurrency issues and real-time constraints, protocols design and design for distributed systems.

    • Database Design

      Learnt about Database system architecture, conceptual database models, relational and object-oriented databases, database system implementation, query processing and optimization, transaction processing concepts, concurrency, and recovery, security.

    • Algorithms and Data Structures

      Studied about Formal specifications and representation of lists, arrays, trees, graphs, multilinked structures, strings and recursive pattern structures, Analysis of associated algorithms, Sorting and searching, file structures.

    • Random Processes

      Learnt about Random processes concepts, Stationarity and independence, Auto-correlation and cross-correlation functions, spectral characteristics, linear systems with random inputs and Special topics with applications.
